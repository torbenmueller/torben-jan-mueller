Biography

Torben Jan Müller is a freelance film composer. He started playing the piano at the age of 6 and had classical lessons for seven years. At the age of 15, he learned guitar as a second instrument. He was taught concert and electric guitar by Roy Last. As lead guitarist, keyboarder and background singer, he has played in various rock and pop bands, including with Anna Depenbusch.

He has worked as a producer and remixer under the artist name Steve Vercetti for various national and international labels, e.g. Peak Hour Music, New York.

Torben Jan Müller studied at the Akademie Deutsche Pop in Cologne and graduated as a film composer, composer, arranger and artistic producer.

Stylistics

Great Symphonic Music, Electronic Music, Rock & Pop, Funk
